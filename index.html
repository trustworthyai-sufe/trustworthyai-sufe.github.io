This is the website of Trustworthy AI Lab of Shanghai University of Finance and Economics. We are a research group focusing on the research of trustworthy AI. We are interested in the following topics: Recommender System, Explainable Recommendation, Privacy-preserving Recommendation, Fairness-aware Recommendation, and so on.
